Privacy & Your Data

This study tool is designed with student privacy as a priority. Here's exactly what happens with your data.

How Your Data is Stored

By default, all your study progress is saved directly in your browser using localStorage. This means:

  • Your data stays on your device
  • Nothing is sent to any server unless cloud sync is enabled
  • Clearing your browser data will reset your progress
  • Your data is specific to the browser and device you're using
If you use the site without entering your name (guest mode), no data leaves your device at all.

Cloud Sync (Optional)

If your teacher has enabled cloud sync (via Supabase), and you enter your name and period, the following data is synced:

Data Why It's Collected
Your first name To identify you on the leaderboard and for your teacher
Your class period To group students by class for the leaderboard and dashboard
Activity progress Flashcard mastery, test scores, and activity completion
Study time Session length and total time for progress tracking
Leaderboard scores Composite score for class rankings (requires teacher approval to appear)

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No student email addresses or passwords (there are no accounts)
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers or fingerprinting
  • No cookies for tracking or advertising
  • No third-party analytics (no Google Analytics, no tracking pixels)
  • No data is sold or shared with anyone outside your classroom

Third-Party Connections

All fonts, icons, and scripts are self-hosted. This site does not make requests to Google, Facebook, or any external analytics or advertising service. No student data is transmitted to third parties.

Who Can See Your Data

  • You can see your own progress on the site
  • Your teacher can see student names, study time, and progress via a private dashboard
  • Your classmates can see first names and scores on the leaderboard (only after teacher approval)
  • No one else has access to the data

Deleting Your Data

You can reset your local progress at any time by clearing your browser's site data. If cloud sync is enabled, ask your teacher to remove your entry from the system.

COPPA & FERPA Compliance

This tool is designed for use in K-12 classrooms. We take student privacy seriously:

  • COPPA: This site collects only a student's first name and class period, which alone do not constitute personal information under COPPA. When cloud sync is enabled in a school setting, the teacher or school acts as the consenting party on behalf of parents under the COPPA school exception. Data is used solely for educational purposes.
  • FERPA: Student progress data (scores, study time) is classified as education records when linked to a student name. Access is limited to the student and their teacher. No data is shared with third parties.
  • Data minimization: We collect only what is needed for the tool to function. No unnecessary personal information is gathered.

Data Retention

Local data (in your browser) persists until you clear your browser's site data. Cloud-synced data is retained for the duration of the school year. Teachers can delete individual student records at any time via the teacher dashboard.

Open Source

This project is open source. Anyone can review exactly how data is handled by reading the code on GitHub. There is nothing hidden.

Questions?

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how student data is handled, please contact your child's teacher or open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
